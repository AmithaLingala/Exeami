This recipe goes great with white rice and potato fry, also tastes good with roti, idly, and dosa.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes – 4 medium size
  • Garlic – handful (15 to 20 cloves)
  • Green chilly – 10 to 12
  • Oil – 2 tbsp
  • Cumin seeds – 1 tsp
  • Mustard seeds – 1 tsp
  • Turmeric – 1 tsp
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Chop green chillies, garlic and tomatoes.(Doesn’t need to be small as we will grind it later)
  2. Add oil in pan and heat it.
  3. Add mustard and cumin seeds.
  4. Add chopped green chilli and garlic.
  5. Keep the flame to medium low and fry them till the colour about to change to brown.
  6. Add some coriander leaves and fry for a 30 sec.
  7. Shift this to a mixer jar.
  8. Add tomatoes and turmeric to the same pan and let them cook with lid closed, till they become soft.
  9. let the tomatoes cool down and then add them to the same mixer jar, add salt and grind them and then sift it to a bowl
  10. Now take pan add a tsp of oil, add cumin seeds, mustard seeds, curry leaves, dried red chilli, 2 to 3 garlic cloves fry them and add it to our chutney.(if you don’t have some of these still our chutney taste great, not to worry)

Tips

Make sure you take equal amount of green chilli and garlic; this makes the recipe tasty.
