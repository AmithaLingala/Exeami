This is a perfect side salad that enhances the flavour of the carrots.

Ingredients

  • Carrots – 2 medium size
  • Lime Juice – 2 tsp (1/2 lime)
  • Maple syrup or honey – 2 tsp
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Grate carrots
  2. Add lime juice, salt, maple syrup and mix well.

Notes

You can add pepper, chopped half onion and coriander leaves if you prefer.
